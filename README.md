# Canopy
Focus your mind

This Mac OS app will allow you to simply focus on your main task at hand by switching your secondary displays (up to 4 total) to a beautiful nature background by triggering a hotkey. There's an optional timer you can also set. The background will switch to a new one daily, or you can manually change it by tapping the arrow in the bottom right of the screen. 

Mac OS Gatekeeper will block this app from opening because it is currently unsigned. 
To override:

Right-click the .app in Finder → Open
Or from Terminal:
xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/Canopy.app
