# CanopyApp
Canopy. Focus your mind

This app will allow you to simply focus on your main task at hand by switching your secondary displays (up to 4 total) to a beautiful nature background by triggering a hotkey. There's an optional timer you can also set. 

Mac OS Gatekeeper will block this app from opening because it is currently unsigned. 
To override:

# Right-click the .app in Finder → Open
# Or from Terminal:
xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/Canopy.app
